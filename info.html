Hi, I am Miraj Shrestha originally from Nepal, after high school currently pursuing my higher studies in Finland. Venturesome but stability loving soul. Someone who feels comfortable around people but is not afraid to be alone either. That’s probably the reason why I feel home both with portrait and nature photography.

I started photography with my first SLR in 2016 and two years later became a part-time entrepreneur. Today I am pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology. Besides photography, I’ve worked in Data Entry companies and led various NGO groups.

Both my work and hobbies have taken me to fascinating places. Lately, I’ve been travelling in different sites of Helsinki. Beauty and adventure can be found close to home, but sometimes, like in Nepal, faraway places feel like home too.

Through my nature photos, I aim to promote environmental awareness and protection. Spending early mornings with wild animals such as foxes, bears, and deer is incredibly rewarding. Studying and photographing them over several days allows me to capture their true essence. I only wish I had more time for that!
